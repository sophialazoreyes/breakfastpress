Nagniningning sa Liwanag

Last November 30, 2018, the PGLU opened the Pasko sa Kapitolyo. This was actually the first time San Fernando La Union opened a Christmas Village. There were lots to see,... there were even some food stalls and other things to witness. I wasn't really expecting it to be as beautiful as it looks like in their page, but as soon as I got to the place, I was just blown away. It really felt like a magical place.



Recently, San Fernando City, La Union launched an Application called PARA where in the means of commuting or travelling has become less stressful. One of the problems that we ,citizens from San Fernando are encountering right now is having a hard time commuting, especially during rush hour. When residents need to commute to any part of the city, they pull out their smartphones, book a ride and wait for a tricycle to fetch them.They now use “Para,” a tricycle booking app developed by Jovan Ortega, president and chief operating officer of Para Software Inc.

Rambak Pamilya 2018

Just last November 24, Ckcians celebrated the annual Rambak Pamilya which was held at Christ the King College, High School Department. Today is the last year I am going to celebrate Rambak Pamilya as a Ckcian.I am going to miss all the events and the also the stalls every time we have our Rambak. Rambak Pamilya symbolizes our school and the whole institution. It makes me unhappy everytime I remember that is going to be my last celebrating not only the Rambak, but also missing all the school events and the memories.
